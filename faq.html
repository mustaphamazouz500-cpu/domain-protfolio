How do I download a modded app? ▼
Navigate to the app or game you want, tap the download button, complete the verification step, and the download link will be provided. The verification helps us keep the service free.
Why do I need to complete a verification? ▼
Verification helps prevent bots and abuse, ensuring that real users get access. It also supports our service so we can continue providing free mods.
Is there a direct download without verification? ▼
Currently, all downloads require verification. This is standard practice among mod stores to maintain the service. VIP users may get priority access in the future.
How do I install an APK on Android? ▼
1. Download the APK file.
2. Go to Settings > Security > Enable "Unknown Sources".
3. Open the downloaded APK and tap "Install".
4. If there's an OBB file, extract it to Android/obb/[package-name]/.
How do I install on iOS (iPhone/iPad)? ▼
iOS mods typically require a third-party installer like AltStore, Sideloadly, or Scarlet. Follow the specific guide provided with each app. No jailbreak is required for most mods.
The app won't install — what should I do? ▼
• Ensure "Unknown Sources" is enabled (Android).
• Check you have enough storage space.
• Disable Play Protect temporarily.
• Make sure you downloaded the correct version for your device.
• Try clearing cache and restarting your device.
Are these mods safe to use? ▼
All mods on ooQleMod are tested before being listed. However, mods are third-party modifications and come with inherent risks. We recommend using a secondary account for testing first.
Can I get banned for using mods? ▼
Yes, there is always a risk of account bans when using modified applications. We recommend using guest/alt accounts for mods, especially in online multiplayer games. ooQleMod is not responsible for any bans.
Do these mods contain viruses? ▼
We scan all files before listing. However, some antivirus programs may flag modded APKs as "riskware" because they are modified. This is usually a false positive. Always download from trusted sources.
Do I need an account to download? ▼
No account is required. Simply browse, select your mod, complete the verification, and download. We don't require registration.
