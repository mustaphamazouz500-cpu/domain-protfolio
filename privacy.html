🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:
• Contact Form Data: Name, email, and message content when you contact us via Formspree.
• Usage Data: Anonymous analytics including page views, device type, and browser information.
• Cookies: Small files to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode).

2. How We Use Your Information

• To respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form.
• To improve our website based on anonymous usage patterns.
• To remember your theme preference for a better experience.
We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies Policy

We use a single functional cookie to remember your dark/light mode preference. This cookie is stored locally in your browser and is not used for tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but the theme preference will not persist.

4. Third-Party Services

Formspree: Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree. Their privacy policy applies to data submitted through the form.
Image CDN: App icons are loaded from third-party CDN servers (pandahelp.vip). These servers may log access requests.
CPA Networks: Download verifications are handled by third-party offer walls which have their own privacy policies.

5. Data Storage & Security

Contact form data is stored securely by Formspree. We do not maintain a separate database of user information. We implement reasonable security measures, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

• Right to request deletion of your data.

Contact us at support@ooqlemod.com to exercise these rights.

8. External Links

Our website contains links to external sites not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

9. Changes to This Policy
